Common First Aid Emergencies

  • Bleeding: Apply pressure, clean the wound, and bandage.
  • Burns: Cool under running water, cover loosely.
  • Choking: Perform abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver).
  • Fainting: Lay the person flat, elevate legs, check breathing.
  • Fractures: Immobilize the area, do not move broken bones.
